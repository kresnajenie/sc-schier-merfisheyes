    Note on Imputed Data
    The displayed expression and chromatin accessibility patterns include computationally imputed predictions derived from integration of single-cell multiome and weMERFISH data. These represent estimated rather than directly measured signals. Users are advised to interpret imputed results with caution, especially for genes with low expression or variability, and to validate findings using measured or independent datasets when possible.
